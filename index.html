What We Do

World Wildlife Fund Canada is the country’s largest international conservation organization. Guided
by the best scientific analysis and Indigenous knowledge, we work to conserve species at risk, protect
threatened habitats, and address climate change. Our long-term vision is simple: to create a world
where nature and people thrive
